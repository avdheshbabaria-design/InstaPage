STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE: 9-GRID EXECUTION

PURPOSE

This document outlines the strict execution pipeline for utilizing the Prodbox Strategy Planner to deploy a 9-grid matrix to a client's social profile. Adherence to this sequence ensures structural integrity, aesthetic consistency, and narrative flow.

PHASE 1: INITIATION & STRATEGY

Define the Concept: Enter the core business narrative into the Prompt Engineer input field located in the top navigation bar.

Generate Strategy: Execute the generation sequence. The system will auto-populate the 9-grid architecture with visual concepts and precise copywriting tailored to the currently active visual preset.

Review & Refine: Select individual panels to review the AI-generated captions. Ensure the tone aligns with the client's brand voice.

PHASE 2: VISUAL DIRECTIVES & SYNTHESIS

Navigate to the Visual Directives module on the left interface.

Select a Preset: Choose from one of the 20 pre-loaded high-end advertising presets.

OR Synthesize a Custom Identity:

Open the Aesthetic Synthesizer.

Use Brand Context to generate a preset based on a well-known brand's established visual identity.

Use Reference Image to upload a moodboard image and reverse-engineer its visual style into a preset.

Synthesized presets are automatically saved and applied to your global directives.

Optional: Engage the High-Contrast Alternation toggle if a checkerboard aesthetic is required for this specific launch.

PHASE 3: ASSET RENDERING

Structural Overrides: Before generating, select specific panels (e.g., Panel 5, The Keystone) and upload any mandatory client assets (product shots, logos) via the "Inject" module under Structural Reference.

Matrix Rendering: Click Render Matrix in the top navigation bar to automatically and sequentially generate art for all 9 panels.

Individual Refinement: If a panel requires adjustment, edit its visual concept text and click Regenerate Canvas on that specific panel.

PHASE 4: COMPILATION & EXPORT

Verify the visual flow of the complete 3x3 matrix in the left simulator.

Click Download Asset on any individual panel to export single pieces of content.

Click Download Grid. The system will compile the individual assets and download the prodbox-9-grid-master.png file to your local machine.

Click Export. This copies the entire sequential text framework (captions, tags, and layout order) to your clipboard.

PHASE 5: DEPLOYMENT (CRITICAL)

WARNING: Grid integrity relies entirely on the upload sequence. You must deploy the assets to the social platform in reverse numerical order.

Execution is now complete. The Framework is live.
